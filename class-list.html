Robotics Class

Robotics introduces young learners to creativity with technology through building and programming. It encourages problem-solving, teamwork, and innovation, helping students gain confidence in science while preparing for a technology-driven future.

Yoga Class

Yoga helps children connect mind and body through simple postures, breathing, and relaxation. It builds focus, calmness, and discipline, giving students a healthy foundation for both academic success and personal growth.

Dance Class

Dance inspires creativity and confidence by teaching rhythm, movement, and cultural expression. From classical to modern styles, it allows students to discover their talents while enjoying fitness, coordination, and artistic growth in school.

Keyboard Class

Keyboard lessons open the world of music with melody, harmony, and rhythm. Students improve memory, focus, and creativity while developing a life-long appreciation for music in a joyful and structured learning environment.
