Our School - Mount Valley Public School

Provides ICSE Curriculum

At Mount Valley Public School, we focus on holistic learning that balances academics, extracurricular activities, and character development. Our ICSE curriculum ensures a strong foundation in core subjects while nurturing creativity and critical thinking.

Join us to experience an environment where every child is encouraged to explore, learn, and excel in all aspects of life.

Why Choose ICSE Curriculum?

Discover the key advantages that make ICSE a great choice for your child's holistic development.

Strong Focus on English

ICSE gives more importance to English language skills, which helps in higher studies in India and abroad.

Balanced Curriculum

Equal importance to sciences, mathematics, humanities, and languages.

Practical Learning

Encourages projects, lab work, and field activities over rote learning.

Wide Subject Choice

Offers subjects like arts, computer science, environmental education, commerce, and more.

Global Recognition

Accepted by universities in India and abroad (especially for English proficiency).

Skill Development

Builds analytical, problem-solving, and research skills.

Focus on Overall Growth

Supports extracurricular activities such as sports, music, drama, and arts.
