Why Mount Valley Public School?

Campus & Buildings

Safe and modern campus with well-ventilated classrooms, proper lighting, and comfortable seating. The school features separate administrative and staff offices along with a principal’s office to ensure smooth functioning. A secure boundary wall, eco-friendly green spaces, and first-aid facilities make it a safe and inspiring environment for learning and growth.

Playground & Sports

The spacious playground supports a variety of sports and physical education activities, encouraging teamwork, fitness, and healthy competition. Students have opportunities to participate in inter-school and intra-school tournaments, while additional activities like yoga, aerobics, and mindfulness help develop overall well-being and concentration. Regular fitness monitoring ensures students maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Academic Excellence

Following the ICSE curriculum, the school emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and holistic learning. Students engage in robotics, STEM projects, arts, music, and spoken English programs to enhance their skills. Project-based learning, practical experiments, and regular assessments ensure academic growth while nurturing confidence, curiosity, and problem-solving abilities in every student.

Dedicated Faculty

Our team of experienced and passionate teachers is committed to the holistic development of every child. They provide personalized attention, mentorship, and guidance while encouraging innovative teaching methods. Continuous training and professional development enable the faculty to stay updated with the latest educational practices, fostering a nurturing and engaging learning environment.

Admission Process

1. Registration

Parents may collect the Application Form from the school office or download it from the school website. The duly filled form must be submitted along with the required documents.

2. Documents Required

Birth Certificate (attested copy), recent passport-size photographs of the student, previous school’s Transfer Certificate (if applicable), previous class Report Card / Progress Record, address proof of parents, and Aadhaar Card copy (if applicable).

3. Eligibility

Nursery/LKG/UKG: Admission based on age criteria as per CISCE guidelines. Class I onwards: Admission is subject to availability of seats and an entrance assessment.

4. Interaction / Entrance Assessment

For Kindergarten: Child and parents may be called for an informal interaction. For Classes I upwards: Students may be required to take an entrance assessment in English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge to evaluate grade readiness.

5. Confirmation of Admission

Admission will be confirmed after successful assessment, verification of documents, and payment of admission/term fees. Parents will receive an Admission Confirmation Letter along with details of uniform, books, and school calendar.

6. Withdrawal

One month’s written notice is required for withdrawal. Transfer Certificate will be issued only after clearance of all dues.
