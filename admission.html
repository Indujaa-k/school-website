Admission Process

Registration

  • ✍️ Collect the application form from the school office
  • 🌐 Or complete the application directly on the school website

Documents Required

  • 📄 Birth Certificate (attested copy)
  • 🖼️ Recent passport-size photographs of the student
  • 🏫 Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • 📚 Report Card / Progress Record
  • 🏠 Address proof of parents
  • 🆔 Aadhaar Card copy (if applicable)

Eligibility

  • 👶 Nursery / LKG / UKG: Based on CISCE age criteria
  • 👥 Class I onwards: Subject to seat availability
  • 📋 Entrance assessment may be required for higher classes

Interaction / Entrance Assessment

  • 💬 Kindergarten: Informal interaction with parents & child
  • 📝 Classes I & above: English test
  • ➗ Mathematics test
  • 🧠 General Knowledge test

Confirmation of Admission

  • ✅ Successful completion of assessment
  • 📑 Verification of submitted documents
  • 💳 Payment of admission and term fees
  • ✉️ Admission Confirmation Letter issued

Withdrawal

  • 📝 One month written notice required
  • 💰 All dues must be cleared
  • 📜 Transfer Certificate issued after clearance
