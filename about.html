Who we are

Mount Valley Public School

Mount Valley Public School was established in [2021] under the guidance of the JM EDUCATIONAL TRUST, with the vision of providing high-quality education that blends knowledge with values. What began as a modest institution with a few classrooms has now grown into a full-fledged ICSE school, nurturing students from the foundational years up to Class X.

Mission

We strive to create a safe, inclusive, and inspiring learning environment where knowledge, values, and skills go hand in hand, preparing students for academic excellence and life beyond the classroom.

Vision

To create a dynamic learning environment that inspires innovation, cultivates leadership, and equips students to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Over the years, the school has consistently focused on academic excellence, holistic development, and character building. With a team of dedicated teachers, modern infrastructure, science and computer laboratories, a well-stocked library, and a spacious playground, the school has become a center of learning and growth.

ICSE Curriculum Overview

The Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE) is conducted by the CISCE, New Delhi. It offers a balanced, application-based, and globally recognized education system.

Language-rich

Strong focus on English + a second language (Hindi/regional/foreign).

Balanced Subjects

Equal weightage to Science, Math, Humanities, and Arts.

Application-based

Focus on understanding, not rote memorization.

Global Recognition

Accepted by top universities in India & abroad.
