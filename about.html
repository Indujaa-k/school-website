Who we are

Mount Valley Public School

Mount Valley Public School was established in 2021 under the guidance of the JM EDUCATIONAL TRUST, with the vision of providing high-quality education that blends knowledge with values. What began as a modest institution with a few classrooms has now grown into a full-fledged ICSE school. Over the years, the school has consistently focused on academic excellence, holistic development, and character building. With a team of dedicated teachers, modern infrastructure, science and computer laboratories, a well-stocked library, and a spacious playground, the school has become a center of learning and growth.

Mission

We strive to create a safe, inclusive, and inspiring learning environment where knowledge, values, and skills go hand in hand, preparing students for academic excellence and life beyond the classroom.

Vision

To create a dynamic learning environment that inspires innovation, cultivates leadership, and equips students to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

ICSE Curriculum Overview

The Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE) is conducted by the CISCE, New Delhi. It offers a balanced, application-based, and globally recognized education system.

Language-rich

Strong focus on English + a second language (Hindi/regional/foreign).

Balanced Subjects

Equal weightage to Science, Math, Humanities, and Arts.

Internal Assessments

20–30% marks from projects and practicals.

Global Recognition

Accepted by top universities in India & abroad.

Subjects Offered

Classes I – VIII (School-based curriculum aligned to ICSE)
  • English (Language + Literature)
  • Second Language (Hindi/regional/foreign)
  • Mathematics
  • Science (General Science → Class VI; Physics, Chemistry, Biology → Class VII+)
  • Social Studies (History, Civics, Geography)
  • Computer Applications / Environmental Studies
  • Art, Craft, Moral Science, Physical Education
Classes IX – X (ICSE Board Exam)

Group I (Compulsory):

  • English (Language + Literature)
  • Second Language
  • History, Civics & Geography

Group II (Choose any 2):

  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Economics / Environmental Science / Commercial Studies
  • Foreign/ Classical Language

Group III (Choose 1):

  • Computer Applications / Economic Applications
  • Art / Performing Arts (Music, Dance, Drama)
  • Physical Education / Environmental Applications
  • Home Science / Technical Drawing / Fashion Designing / Yoga / Cookery
🏆 Assessment System

Class IX → School exams (CISCE pattern).

Class X → ICSE Board Exam (CISCE).

Marks Split:

  • 80% Written Exam
  • 20% Internal Assessment / Project Work
